Features

Stop-by visit

A sitter visits the owner's house several times a day to quickly check the status of the pet and pet.

Example: Dog to take care of, Cat and pet in gauge, Help with small housework

Caretaking

Sitter visits owner's house and takes care of pets such as toilet, walk, play.

Example: Baby pets and energetic pets, Pets not good at loneliness, Pets that need special care such as aging, Family who is late for work

Dog walking

Sitter visits owner's house and walks for pet.

Example: Dog that needs a walk
